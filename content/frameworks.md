| Framework | Author(s) | Core Idea | Used In |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Switch** | Chip & Dan Heath | Direct the Rider (rational), Motivate the Elephant (emotional), Shape the Path (environment) | Rules 1, 2, 3 |
| **Nudge** | Richard Thaler & Cass Sunstein | Defaults, social proof, and choice architecture drive behavior more than information | Rules 2, 7, Exec |
| **8-Step Change Model** | John Kotter | Change requires urgency, coalition, vision, communication, empowerment, wins, consolidation, anchoring | Rule 8 |
| **Transitions Model** | William Bridges | Change is external; transition is internal. Every transition starts with an Ending. | Rules 5, 6 |
| **Change Model (Unfreeze-Change-Refreeze)** | Kurt Lewin | Destabilize current state, implement change, stabilize new state | Rules 3, 8 |
| **Tipping Point Leadership** | W. Chan Kim & Renee Mauborgne | Concentrate resources on disproportionate influence factors to break through inertia | Rule 3 |
| **WOOP** | Gabriele Oettingen | Wish → Outcome → Obstacle → Plan. Mental contrasting + implementation intentions | Rule 7 |
| **Radical Change** | Marc Schabracq | Continuous adaptation strategies for unstable environments | Rules 4, 8 |
| **ADKAR** | Prosci (Jeff Hiatt) | Awareness → Desire → Knowledge → Ability → Reinforcement | Rule 8 |
| **DxGxFxP** | Beckhard & Harris | Dissatisfaction x Goals x First Steps must exceed Pain of change | Rule 1 |
